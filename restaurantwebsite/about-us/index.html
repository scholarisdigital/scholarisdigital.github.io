About Us.

We have been serving succulent lechon belly in the lechon business industry for a few years. We are proud to bring our rich heritage and unique taste to Melbourne. Our goal is to give all our customers an exceptional culinary experience. We want to share the delicious and cultural Filipino cuisine to everyone in the diverse community of Melbourne. You can taste our unrelenting commitment to quality, service and authenticity in every bite of our lechon belly. Come and try our signature dish that’s uniquely Filipino, here in Melbourne.

A Few Words About Us

Serving Filipino food Since 1998

Melbourne is a vibrant and diverse city with many different cultural communities residing within it. In this particular area of Melbourne, a significant number of people with Filipino heritage have made it their home. These individuals bring with them a rich culture and history that adds to the fabric of the community. From delicious cuisine to colorful festivals and traditions, the presence of the Filipino community is celebrated and warmly welcomed by all. This area is truly an exciting and dynamic place to be, with opportunities to explore new cultures, make connections, and build friendships with people from all walks of life. Whether you are a resident of Melbourne or simply visiting, be sure to take the time to learn about and experience the unique contributions and perspectives of the Filipino community in this area.
